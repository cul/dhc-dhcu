Michelle Chesner

Michelle Chesner is the Norman E. Alexander Librarian for Jewish Studies at Columbia. Her research focus is on the history of early Hebrew printing. Creator of #dhjewish, she also created the first organized list of Jewish Studies DH projects, which can now be found here. Michelle is now involved in various digital humanities projects at Columbia and abroad.
