Stefaan Van Liefferinge

Stefaan van Liefferinge is the current director of Columbiaâ€™s Media Center for Art History. He specializes in the study of architecture from the perspective of the history of science in the Middle Ages. His doctoral dissertation researched the use of mathematical knowledge for the design of the Gothic choir of the cathedral of Notre-Dame of Paris. Besides his PhD in art history and archaeology, Van Liefferinge earned degrees in computer science and physics, and has previously worked for IT companies. He has been involved in different research projects involving the digital media and is committed to investigate new ways to study the monuments of the past using advanced technologies.
