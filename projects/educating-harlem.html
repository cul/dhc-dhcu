Educating Harlem

Educating Harlem is collaborating with the Columbia Center for Digital Research and Scholarship and the Columbia Rare Book & Manuscript Library to create a digital archive site that makes accessible digitized manuscript materials, oral histories, and other materials related to the history of schooling and learning in Harlem.
